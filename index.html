About me

I am currently a post doctoral researcher at ENS Lyon under the supervision of Rémi Gribonval. I am currently working on compressive learning and sketching methods. Before that, I was a Ph.D. at CRIStAL under the supervision of Pierre Chainais and Rémi Bardenet. I was previously working on leveraging determinantal point processes (DPPs) for design problems with provable guarantees: column subset selection for low rank approximations and nodes design for quadrature and interpolation in RKHSs.
