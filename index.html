About me

I am currently a postdoc at ENS Lyon, working with Rémi Gribonval. Before that, I was a Ph.D. at CRIStAL under the supervision of Pierre Chainais and Rémi Bardenet. I am currently interested in leveraging determinantal point processes (DPPs) for design problems with provable guarantees: column subset selection for low rank approximations and nodes design for quadratures and interpolations in RKHS.
